# Frequently Asked Questions

---

## General

### What is Craft & Culture?

Craft & Culture is a platform connecting wine partners with the UAE/GCC market. We handle the logistics of importing wine through licensed distributors, making it easy for you to sell to private clients and businesses in the region.

### Who can use the Partner Portal?

Wine partners (producers, négociants, importers) who have been onboarded by Craft & Culture. If you're interested in becoming a partner, contact us at partners@craftculture.xyz.

### What regions do you cover?

Currently we operate in:
- UAE (primary market)
- Other GCC countries (expanding)

## Orders

### What is a PCO order?

**PCO** stands for **Private Client Order**. These are orders for individual consumers who want to purchase wine for personal use in the UAE.

### What is local inventory?

Local inventory is wine already in the UAE Freezone warehouse. Ordering from local stock means faster delivery (1-2 weeks vs 3-6 weeks for shipped orders).

### How long does an order take?

Typical timeline:
- Review: 1-3 days
- Payment processing: 1-2 weeks
- Delivery: 1-3 weeks after payment

**Total:** 3-6 weeks from submission to delivery

Airfreight orders are faster; ocean freight takes longer.

### Can I cancel an order?

**Before approval:** Yes, you can cancel from the order page.

**After approval:** Contact C&C - cancellation may not be possible if:
- Payment has been collected
- Stock has been shipped

### What happens if a product is out of stock?

C&C will notify you and offer options:
- Wait for restock
- Substitute with alternative
- Partial fulfillment
- Cancel the item

## Pricing

### How is pricing calculated?

Your **Landed Duty Free (LDF)** price is the base. UAE costs are added:
- Import Duty (20%)
- Logistics (transfer from Freezone)
- VAT (5%)

The client pays the final total.

### What do I get paid?

You receive your LDF price for the products. The UAE costs (duty, VAT) are collected from the client but handled by the distributor.

### Can I set my own prices?

Your catalog has standard prices. For custom arrangements:
- Use the B2B Calculator to add margins
- Contact C&C for special pricing

### How often do prices update?

Prices sync from CultX and can update:
- When exchange rates change
- When supplier prices change
- When you request changes

## Payments

### When do I get paid?

After the distributor pays C&C (typically 2-3 weeks after client payment).

### How do I get paid?

Bank transfer to your registered account. Contact C&C to update payment details.

### What currency are payments in?

Payments are typically in USD. Contact C&C for other currency arrangements.

### Can I see payment history?

Yes - filter your orders by "Partner Paid" status to see completed payments.

## Products

### How do I add products to my catalog?

Products are synced from CultX. Contact C&C to:
- Add new products
- Update existing listings
- Remove discontinued items

### Can I request products not in my catalog?

Yes - use the "Request Product" feature when creating an order. C&C will source it and provide pricing.

Contact C&C with:
- Product name
- What's incorrect
- Correct information

## Clients

### Can I see client information?

You see:
- Client name
- Contact details (for verification)
- Delivery address

You don't see:
- Payment details
- Internal pricing margins

### Do I contact the client directly?

No - the distributor handles client communication for:
- Payment collection
- Delivery scheduling
- Customer service

You may contact clients for verification purposes only.

### What if a client has issues?

Direct them to the distributor, or:
1. Note the issue on the order
2. Contact C&C support
3. We coordinate resolution

## Verification

### What is client verification?

Some distributors require verification that the client is registered in their system before processing orders.

### Why is verification needed?

UAE regulations require:
- Licensed distributors for alcohol sales
- Verified customers
- Proper documentation

### What do I need to do?

When prompted:
1. Confirm the client is known to you
2. Confirm they're registered with the distributor
3. Click "Confirm Verification"

## Glossary

|------|------------|
| **C&C** | Craft & Culture |
| **PCO** | Private Client Order |
