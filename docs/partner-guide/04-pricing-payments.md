# Pricing & Payments

Understanding how prices are calculated and when you get paid.

---

## Price Breakdown

When you create an order, you'll see a price breakdown:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  ORDER PRICING                                                      │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                                     │
│  Subtotal (Landed Duty Free)              $1,550.00                │
│  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────                │
│  Import Duty (20%)                          $310.00                │
│  Logistics                                   $11.63                │
│  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────                │
│  VAT (5%)                                    $93.58                │
│  ══════════════════════════════════════════════════                │
│  TOTAL (Client Price)                     $1,965.21                │
│                                                                     │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### What Each Line Means

| Line | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Subtotal (LDF)** | Your Landed Duty Free price - what the wine costs before UAE import |
| **Import Duty** | UAE import tax (20% of subtotal) |
| **Logistics** | Cost of moving wine from Freezone to Mainland UAE |
| **VAT** | UAE Value Added Tax (5%) |
| **Total** | Final price the client pays |

---

## Landed Duty Free (LDF)

The **Subtotal** shows your Landed Duty Free price. This is:
- The price of wine delivered to UAE Freezone
- Before any import duty is paid
- Before transfer to Mainland UAE

```
  YOUR PRICE                  UAE COSTS                    CLIENT PRICE
 ┌───────────┐            ┌─────────────────┐            ┌─────────────┐
 │           │            │  Import Duty    │            │             │
 │   LDF     │ ─────────▶ │  Logistics      │ ─────────▶ │   TOTAL     │
 │           │            │  VAT            │            │             │
 └───────────┘            └─────────────────┘            └─────────────┘
   $1,550                    + $415.21                      $1,965.21
```

## Currency

All prices are shown in **USD** and **AED**:

| Currency | Rate | Notes |
|----------|------|-------|
| USD | Base | All calculations done in USD |
| AED | 3.67 per USD | Fixed rate (pegged currency) |

The client typically pays in AED.

## Your Payment

### What You Receive

You are paid your **Landed Duty Free** price for the products.

**Example:**
- Order subtotal (LDF): $1,550.00
- You receive: **$1,550.00**

The duty, logistics, and VAT are collected from the client but don't come to you.

### Payment Status Tracking

You can see payment progress in the order detail:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Awaiting Client Payment** | Distributor collecting from client |
| **Client Paid** | Client has paid |
| **Awaiting Distributor Payment** | Distributor processing payment to C&C |
| **Distributor Paid** | C&C received funds |
| **Awaiting Partner Payment** | Your payment being processed |
| **Partner Paid** | ✓ You've been paid |

Go to **Orders** and filter by "Partner Paid" to see completed payments.

Each order shows:
- Order total (client price)
- Your amount (LDF)
- Payment date
- Payment reference

## Pricing Questions

### Why don't I see the full breakdown?

You see the client-facing breakdown:
- Subtotal (your LDF price)
- Import Duty
- Logistics
- VAT
- Total

This is what the client pays. Internal margins are handled by C&C.

### Can I offer discounts?

Contact C&C if you want to adjust pricing for a specific client or order. Special pricing can be arranged for:
- Large orders
- Repeat clients
- Special promotions

### What if exchange rates change?

- GBP and EUR rates are updated regularly
- USD to AED is fixed at 3.67 (currency peg)
- Orders are priced at the rate when submitted

## B2B vs PCO Pricing

| Aspect | B2B Quote | PCO Order |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| **Client Type** | Business | Individual |
| **Pricing** | Simpler (your price + margin) | Full UAE costs added |
| **Duty/VAT** | Business handles | Included in total |
| **Your Payment** | Same LDF approach | Same LDF approach |
