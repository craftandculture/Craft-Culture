# Getting Started

## Logging In

1. Go to [craftculture.xyz](https://craftculture.xyz)
2. Click **Sign In**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for the magic link
5. Click the link to access your dashboard

> **Note:** We use passwordless login for security. You'll receive a new magic link each time you sign in.

---

### Dashboard Sections

| Section | What It Shows |
|---------|---------------|
| **Orders** | Your active PCO orders and their status |
| **Quotes** | B2B quotes you've created |
| **Products** | Your products in the catalog |
| **Activity** | Recent updates on your orders |

---

## Navigation

The main menu gives you access to:

| Menu Item | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Dashboard** | Overview and recent activity |
| **Orders** | Create and manage PCO orders |
| **Products** | View your product catalog |
| **Inventory** | See local UAE stock for fast delivery |
| **Settings** | Your account preferences |

---

## Account Settings

Access your settings by clicking your profile icon (top right).

You can:
- Update your profile information
- Change notification preferences
- Switch between light/dark theme
- Sign out
