## Product Information

### What You See

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Name** | Wine name and vintage |
| **Producer** | Winery or producer name |
| **Region** | Wine region and country |
| **Type** | Red, White, Rosé, Sparkling, etc. |
| **Pack** | Case configuration (bottles × size) |
| **Price** | Your Landed Duty Free price per case |
| **Stock** | Availability status |

### Case Configurations

Wines come in different case configurations:

| Pack | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| 12 × 750ml | 12 standard bottles per case |
| 6 × 750ml | 6 standard bottles per case |
| 6 × 1.5L | 6 magnums per case |
| 3 × 750ml | 3 bottles per case |
| 1 × 750ml | Single bottle |

## Adding Products to Orders

When creating an order:

1. Click **Add Product**
2. Search for the wine
3. Select from your catalog
4. Enter quantity (cases)
5. Click **Add**

The product is added to your order with current pricing.

## Special Requests

Need a wine not in your catalog?

### Requesting a Product

1. In the order form, click **Request Product**
2. Describe what you need:
   - Wine name and vintage
   - Producer
   - Approximate quantity
   - Any other details

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  REQUEST PRODUCT                                                    │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                                     │
│  Wine Name:    [Petrus 2015____________________________]            │
│                                                                     │
│  Producer:     [Petrus________________________________]             │
│                                                                     │
│  Quantity:     [2 cases_______________________________]             │
│                                                                     │
│  Notes:                                                             │
│  [Client specifically wants 2015 vintage if available ]            │
│                                                                     │
│  [Submit Request]                                                   │
│                                                                     │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### What Happens Next

1. C&C team receives your request
2. They source the wine
3. If available, they provide pricing
4. You approve and add to order

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why can't I see a product?

- It may not be in your allocated catalog
- Check if filters are hiding it
- It may be out of stock
- Contact C&C to add products

### Why did the price change?

Prices can change due to:
- Currency exchange rate updates
- Supplier price changes
- Market conditions

Orders are locked at the price when submitted.

### Can I set my own prices?

Your catalog shows standard LDF prices. For custom pricing arrangements:
- Contact C&C team
- Special rates can be configured for your account
