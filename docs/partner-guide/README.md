## Platform Overview

Craft & Culture connects wine partners with the UAE/GCC market through licensed distributors. As a partner, you can:

- Submit orders for private clients (PCO)
- Track order progress from submission to delivery
- View local UAE inventory for faster delivery
- Manage your product catalog
- View payment status

### Key Terms

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **PCO** | Private Client Order - orders for individual consumers |
| **B2B** | Business-to-Business quotes for trade customers |
| **Distributor** | Licensed UAE company that handles final delivery |
| **C&C** | Craft & Culture - reviews and coordinates orders |
| **LDF** | Landed Duty Free - price before UAE import costs |
