# Inventory

View available stock in UAE for faster delivery.

---

## What is Local Inventory?

Local inventory is wine already in the UAE - stored in our bonded warehouse (Freezone). This means:

| Benefit | Why It Matters |
|---------|----------------|
| **Faster delivery** | No shipping wait - stock is here |
| **Lower logistics cost** | No air/ocean freight needed |
| **Confirmed availability** | Physical stock, not estimated |

| Field | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Available** | Can be ordered now |

| **Incoming** | On the water/in air, arriving soon |
| **Total** | All stock (current + incoming) |

## Filtering Inventory

### Filter Options

| Filter | Use For |
|--------|---------|
| **Status** | Show only in-stock, or include incoming |
| **Region** | Bordeaux, Burgundy, etc. |
| **Type** | Red, White, Sparkling |
| **Price Range** | Min - Max per case |
| **Partner** | Your products only |

- Wine name
- Producer

- Product details
- Quantity
- Estimated arrival (ETA)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why doesn't my product show in inventory?

- It may not be stocked locally yet
- Check your full catalog for shipping options
- Request C&C to stock it locally

### Can I request stock to be held?

Contact C&C for allocation requests on high-demand items.

### How accurate is the stock count?

Inventory syncs with our warehouse management system (Logilize). Counts are updated in real-time.

### What if stock sells out while I'm ordering?

The system checks availability when you submit. If stock is gone, you'll be notified to adjust quantities.
