1. Overview

The Warehouse Management System (WMS) helps you manage your physical warehouse locations, track stock positions, and handle warehouse operations like receiving, put-away, and transfers.

Location Naming Convention

Locations follow the format: AISLE-BAY-LEVEL

Example Aisle Bay Level Description
A-01-00 A 01 00 Aisle A, Bay 1, Floor level
A-01-02 A 01 02 Aisle A, Bay 1, Level 2 (forklift)
B-03-01 B 03 01 Aisle B, Bay 3, Level 1
Level 00 is the floor level (no forklift needed). Levels 01 and above typically require a forklift.
